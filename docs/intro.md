## About Physical AI

Physical AI represents a paradigm shift from traditional AI that operates purely in digital spaces to AI that must understand and interact with the physical world. Unlike chatbots or recommendation systems, Physical AI must:

- **Perceive** the real world through sensors
- **Understand** physical laws and constraints
- **Act** in three-dimensional space with real consequences
- **Learn** from physical interactions and experiences

Humanoid robotics takes this concept further by creating robots with human-like form and capabilities, enabling more natural interaction with human environments and tools.
